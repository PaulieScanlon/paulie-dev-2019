Gatsby recently announced the launch of “functions”. In this article, Paul Scanlon explains how to get the current
location of the International Space Station (ISS) as it orbits the Earth in realtime using
[Gatsby Serverless Functions](https://www.gatsbyjs.com/docs/reference/functions/) and then display it on a 3D
interactive globe using [React Three Fibre](https://github.com/pmndrs/react-three-fiber).
